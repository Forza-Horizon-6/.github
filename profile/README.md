## About Forza Horizon 6

Forza Horizon 6 is an open-world racing game that delivers high-speed driving across large and diverse environments. Players can explore expansive maps, participate in races, complete events, and customize a wide range of vehicles while progressing through a dynamic racing experience.

The game focuses on freedom, allowing players to drive anywhere, discover events, and engage in different racing modes. It features realistic driving physics combined with accessible controls, making it suitable for both casual and experienced players.

With a large car collection, visual upgrades, multiplayer events, and evolving environments, Forza Horizon 6 offers a racing experience built around exploration, competition, and customization.
